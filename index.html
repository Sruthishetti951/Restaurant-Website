Pan Cake

Description: Our fluffy and delicious pancakes are a breakfast favorite, perfect for starting your day on a high note. Made with wholesome ingredients, they offer a delightful balance of taste and nutrition.

Health Benefits: Pancakes, especially when made with whole grains, can be a good source of essential nutrients. They provide a balanced amount of carbohydrates for energy, proteins for muscle repair, and healthy fats for sustained fullness. Adding toppings like fresh fruits can increase the vitamin and fiber content, contributing to better digestion and overall health.

Calories: A standard serving of pancakes (approximately 3 medium-sized pancakes) can range between 200-350 calories, depending on the ingredients used. For a healthier option, choosing whole grain or gluten-free flour can help reduce the calorie count and add more fiber to your meal.

Cake

Description: Indulge in our delightful cakes, where each bite offers a perfect blend of flavor and texture. Our cakes are crafted with high-quality ingredients to ensure a satisfying treat that's as pleasing to your health as it is to your palate.

Health Benefits: While cakes are often considered a treat, they can be part of a balanced diet when enjoyed in moderation and made with healthier ingredients. Cakes can offer a quick source of energy and can be enriched with nutrient-dense components like fruits, nuts, and whole grains.

Calories: The calorie content of cakes varies widely depending on the recipe and portion size. On average, a slice of cake can range from 200-500 calories. Opting for smaller portions and cakes made with healthier ingredients can help manage calorie intake.
